Lucid Feature #22 · star & review

Star a word or translation — review it later

Two new surfaces. WI-3 — the star toggle sits on the word-lookup popover (a single word) and on the translate / polish result panes (a whole sentence). WI-4 — the “Starred” review tab joins Sessions & Glossary in the sidebar: a searchable list of everything you've starred, with per-item detail and an unstar action. This bundle specifies every state, the light/dark tokens, the phone drawer, RTL, and CJK sources. The starredStore + sync entity are built headless and have no design dependency.
