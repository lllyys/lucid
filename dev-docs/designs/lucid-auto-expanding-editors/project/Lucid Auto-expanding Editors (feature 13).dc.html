Auto-expanding editor panes — design bundle

Editors grow to fit their content instead of scrolling internally. The committed bundle depicts fixed flex-share + inner scroll; this changes the sizing model — editors size to content, the panel column reflows and scrolls, and only a max-height cap restores an inner scroll for huge pastes.

…dominates the frame budget. We profiled the deferred pass across three GPUs and the pattern held: the lighting resolve and the gbuffer fill each pay a full bandwidth round-trip. Folding them removes one. The risk is the transparency pass, which currently reads the resolved buffer — so we gate the fold behind a capability check and fall back on older hardware. Early numbers show a 19% drop in p95 frame time on the mid-tier card, larger on the laptop SKU where memory bandwidth is the binding constraint…
