wiped by reload). The decisive proof is the deterministic high-fidelity test at the real subsystem boundaries:
the **controller** adopts on equal-rev (`apply(CONFIG_B)`), and the **real `defaultProviderConfigAdapter`**
writes those keys into the **real `useProviderStore`** â€” together the full restore chain (unlock â†’ adopt â†’

CDP run verified the surrounding lifecycle (set-passphrase â†’ blob, reload â†’ locked, unlock) while the
key-restore link is proven by the deterministic tests above.
